My journey so far


I am Mile Karovski, retrained UX/UI Designer. I have a Bachelor's degree in Music and 12 years of experience as a professional stage musician, composer and mentor. I have completed a number of projects with various teams and won several awards in international competitions. After 12 years in the music industry I need a change and have a desire to find a more secure and creative outlet for myself.

My experience with music helps me understand the processes of a UX/UI design. In fact, I've found that the processes are the same: We have deadlines, project understanding, research, teamwork (sometimes individual), target audience, we want to convey a clear message or solve a specific problem, prototyping, testing, etc.
All of these processes are familiar to me and help me solve design projects. My 8 years of experience as a mentor helps me a lot and gives me the opportunity to develop deep empathy. I can easily and quickly put myself in the shoes of the user.

I see myself as someone who never stops learning. I am a big picture thinker, obsessed with working out the details. I am personable and an energetic collaborator. Ambitious, diplomatic & adaptable to the task at hand. I am always trying to grow and learn something new. I am a team player, [working with different teams on different projects as a musician has made me a great team player]. I have a deep sense of empathy. I'm willing to experiment and I'm not afraid to be wrong.

I believe that equal access to information and technology has the power to create a more just and inclusive world.
